Before you start

The process for rotating each certificate is slightly different. Read the steps on each page carefully, even if you’ve rotated certificates before.

Before you upload your Verify Service Provider (VSP) encryption certificate, you must:

  • Create a new encryption key and certificate.
  • Add the new key and certificate to your VSP configuration.
  • Restart the VSP to apply the changes.

Find out more about rotating your VSP encryption key and certificate in the documentation.
