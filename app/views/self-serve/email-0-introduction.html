Hello [DVSA team],

The GOV.​UK Verify team has been improving the process for managing your certificates and keys, and has launched a new platform that will allow you to update them independently.

To access the platform, you’ll need to set up an account by following this link: https://www.verify.service.gov.uk/create-account

To find out more about the new process, read our guidance at https://www.docs.verify.service.gov.uk/maintain-your-connection/rotate-keys/

If you have any concerns or feedback on the new certificate and key management process, you can email the team at idasupport@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk
