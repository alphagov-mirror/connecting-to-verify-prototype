Hello Universal Credit team,

The GOV.​UK Verify team has been improving the process for managing your keys and certificates.

The new process allows you to update your keys and certificates without needing support from the GOV.UK Verify team.

You can now:

  • sign your own certificates
  • upload them to the new GOV.UK Verify Service Admin tool

To access the GOV.UK Service Admin tool, you’ll need to create an account.

Read guidance on the new process.

If you have any concerns or feedback on the new certificate and key management process, you can email the team at idasupport@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk

Best wishes,
GOV.UK Verify
