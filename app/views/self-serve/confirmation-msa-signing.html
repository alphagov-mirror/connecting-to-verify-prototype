Next steps

  • Restart the MSA to apply the changes.
  • Once you've received the email, delete the old signing key and certificate from your MSA configuration.
  • Restart the MSA to apply the changes.
  • Tell GOV.UK Verify to stop using your old (or 'secondary') certificate using this tool.
