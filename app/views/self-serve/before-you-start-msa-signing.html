Before you start

To update your signing certificate:

  • Create a new signing certificate and key.
  • Upload your new signing certificate for your Matching Service Adapter to the GOV.UK Verify Service Admin tool.
  • Deploy the new signing key to your Matching Service Adapter.

For more information, read the guidance on rotating your MSA signing key.
