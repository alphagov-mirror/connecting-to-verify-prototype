Before you upload your Matching Service Adapter (MSA) certificate, you must:

  • Create a new encryption key and certificate.
  • Add your new encryption key and certificate to your MSA configuration.
  • Restart the MSA to apply the changes

Find out more about rotating your MSA encryption key and certificate in the documentation.
