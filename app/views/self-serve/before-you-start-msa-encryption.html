Before you start

To update your encryption certificate:

  • Create a new encryption certificate and key.
  • Deploy the new encryption key to your Matching Service Adapter. If your service supports dual running, you should also deploy a secondary encryption key.
  • Upload your new encryption certificate for your Matching Service Adapter to the GOV.UK Verify Service Admin tool.
