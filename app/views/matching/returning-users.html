Does your service need to recognise returning users?

Users will need to use some services more often than others.

For example, some users will have to file a Self Assessment tax return every year. This service needs to remember who its users are so they don't have to submit the same information about themselves every time they use it.

Users can only do other things once, such as claim their State Pension. This service doesn't need to remember who its users are because they won't have to use it again.
