Find out how identities are matched

The GOV.UK Verify team will decide how your service should match identities with records, as well as what counts as a successful match.

They'll also tell you what to do when:

  • an identity is successfully matched to a record in your database
  • an identity is matched to more than one record in your database
  • no match is found

This is known as a 'matching strategy'. Contact the GOV.UK Verify team to get your matching strategy.
