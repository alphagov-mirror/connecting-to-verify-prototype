Check the quality of your data

Before you start matching identities with records, you must check the data is good enough to use. It'll be more difficult to find a match if the data you're using is out of date or incomplete.

The data should:

  • contain accurate data from a reliable source
  • include the user's name and date of birth
  • cover the right demographic for your service, for example a database of 18 to 25 year olds won't be useful for a service that lets users claim their State Pension

It shouldn't:

  • contain duplicate records or multiple records for the same user
  • contain things like symbols or special characters

Find out what you need to do to prepare the data before you start looking for matches.
