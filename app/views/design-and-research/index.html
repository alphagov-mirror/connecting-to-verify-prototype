Design and research your service Design your service using GOV.UK Verify

You service should send users to GOV.UK Verify at the point you need to check who they are. This is usually near the start of the user journey.

The user will choose a certified company to verify their identity. Once they've been verified, they'll be sent back to your service. You'll be able to see their name, address and date of birth.

Email connect-to-verify-prototype@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk if you have any questions about how GOV.UK Verify fits into your service.

GOV.UK Verify will show any users who can't be verified the other ways they can use your service, for example by phone or by post.
