Build your service to work with GOV.UK Verify

To connect to Verify, you will need the right team in place and have a plan for how you will build your service.

Have the right team

Your service must be able to send SAML (Security Assertion Markup Language) requests to, and receive SAML responses from, the GOV.UK Verify hub. You will need someone on your team who can work with SAML code.

Decide how your service will match identities

Next steps

A developer on your team needs to create an account to connect to Verify. This account will keep track of your progress and store important keys and certificates.
