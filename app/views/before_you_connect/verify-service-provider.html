Build your service Safely exchange information with GOV.UK Verify

GOV.UK Verify uses Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML) to safely exchange information about users with your service.

The Verify Service Provider is a piece of software that helps your service communicate with GOV.UK Verify. It does this by converting:

  • JSON requests you send to GOV.UK Verify into SAML
  • SAML responses GOV.UK Verify sends to your service to JSON

This means you don't need to be familiar with SAML to use GOV.UK Verify in your service. You only need to know how to use JSON.

Download the Verify Service Provider and follow the instructions to configure and run it. You need Java 8 to do this.
