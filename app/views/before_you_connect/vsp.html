Safely exchange information with GOV.UK Verify

GOV.UK Verify uses Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML) to safely send information about users to your service.

You can use the Verify Service Provider to:

  • make SAML requests
  • translate SAML responses
  • send SAML requests to GOV.UK Verify and receive responses

The Verify Service Provider also makes it easier to:

  • connect more than one service to GOV.UK Verify
  • host certificates

Download the Verify Service Provider and follow the instructions to configure and run it.
