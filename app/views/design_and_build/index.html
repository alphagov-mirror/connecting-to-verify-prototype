Design and test your service using GOV.UK Verify

Where GOV.UK Verify fits in your service’s user journey

Your service should send the user to GOV.UK Verify at the point you need to check who they are. The user will choose a certified company to verify their identity.

Once they’ve been verified, the user will be sent back to the service. You’ll be able to see their name, address and date of birth.

See how GOV.UK Verify works with a LOA 1 service and LOA 2 service.

Describe other ways to use your service

When you connect your service to GOV.UK, you’ll be asked to describe the other ways users can use your service, for example by post or by phone.

This will be shown to any users that can’t be verified by a certified company.
