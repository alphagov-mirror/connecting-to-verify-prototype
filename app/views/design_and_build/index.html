Design your service to work with GOV.UK Verify

The GOV.UK Verify user journey

  • Your service sends it’s user’s to GOV.UK Verify.
  • They choose who checks their identity.
  • Once they’ve been checked GOV.UK Verify returns them to your service. You can access their name, date of birth and address.
    View the journey for a low risk service
    View the journey for a higher risk service

Services that need additional assurance

Some services, for example, ‘Get a Passwort’ need to be more certain the user is who they say they are. Something about how to get to a higher level of assurance and maybe examples.

When a user can’t be successfully verified

If a user can’t successfully verify they’ll need to be able to send you additional identification.
