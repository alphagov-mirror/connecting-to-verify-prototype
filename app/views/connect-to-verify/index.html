Get access to environments

Once you've built and hosted your service, you can ask for access to the Integration and Production environments. The GOV.UK Verify team will review your request.

Use the Integration environment to test your service's end-to-end user journey. The Integration environment doesn't use information about real users or link to real certified companies' websites.

You should only ask for access to the Production environment when your service is ready to go live.
