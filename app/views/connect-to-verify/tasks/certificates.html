Get certificates

To connect to Production, you need to generate 4 private keys and corresponding certificate signing requests. Then you need to get them signed by GOV.UK Verify. They are:

  • an encryption certificate for your service
  • a signing certificate for your service
  • an encryption certificate for your Matching Service Adapter
  • a signing certificate for your Matching Service Adapter

Send certificate signing requests

Once you have generated all 4 certificate signing requests, you need to send them to GOV.UK Verify to be signed.
